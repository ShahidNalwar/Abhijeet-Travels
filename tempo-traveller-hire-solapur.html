Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tempo traveller hire in Solapur.

Tempo Traveller hire in Solapur starts at ₹25/km for 9, 12 and 13 seater. 17 and 20 seater Force Urbania is ₹26/km. 26 seater is ₹32/km. 40 seater mini bus is ₹50/km. 45 seater coach is ₹55/km and 49 seater is ₹58/km. Rates are inclusive of driver, fuel, toll and parking. Minimum km charges may apply.

Abhijeet Travels offers 9, 12, 13, 17, 20, 26, 40, 45 and 49 seater options. Small tempo travellers (9–13 seater) are ideal for family outings and small groups. Force Urbania (17–26 seater) is best for pilgrimages and corporate trips. 40–49 seater coaches are available for very large groups.

Yes, all our tempo travellers are available for outstation trips from Solapur. Popular destinations include Pandharpur, Akkalkot, Tuljapur, Shirdi, Pune, Mumbai, Nashik, Aurangabad, Goa and Hyderabad. Multi-day tours with overnight stays are also arranged.

Yes, for round trip and day tour bookings the driver waits at the destination at no extra charge. This is especially useful for Pandharpur Vitthal darshan, Akkalkot Swami Samarth darshan and Tuljapur Bhavani Mata darshan where waiting time can be 2–4 hours.

For regular days, booking 2–3 days in advance is sufficient. During Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi, Navratri, Diwali and New Year, we recommend booking 7–15 days in advance as demand is very high during these periods.

Our per-km rate includes driver allowance, fuel, toll charges and parking fees. For inter-state trips (e.g. Solapur to Hyderabad), state border tax is extra and paid directly at the checkpost. GST is also additional and communicated upfront. No hidden charges — the quoted price is the final price.
