Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Solapur to Hyderabad cab booking.

A Sedan (Dzire/Amaze) starts at ₹5,200 one-way and ₹8,200 round trip. SUV (Ertiga) starts at ₹6,500 one-way and ₹9,300 round trip. Premium SUV (Innova/Crysta) starts at ₹8,800 one-way and ₹12,650 round trip. Prices exclude toll and parking.

Solapur to Hyderabad is approximately 320 km by road via NH-65. The journey typically takes 5 to 6 hours under normal traffic conditions — one of the smoother inter-state highways with less congestion.

The cab travels via NH-65 through Gulbarga (Kalaburagi), then continues via Yadgir and Raichur into Telangana. It's a direct, well-maintained national highway. You can be dropped anywhere in Hyderabad — Hitech City, Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Secunderabad, Old City, etc.

Yes, since this is an inter-state trip crossing from Maharashtra into Telangana, a state border tax applies and is paid directly at the border checkpoint. This is not included in the base fare and is a standard government levy.

Yes, round trip cabs are available. A Sedan round trip starts at ₹8,200, SUV at ₹9,300 and Innova at ₹12,650. Specify your return date while booking for guaranteed availability.

For 1–4 passengers, the Sedan (Dzire/Amaze) is the most economical. For families of 5–6, the SUV (Ertiga) is ideal for the long drive. For maximum comfort on this 320 km journey, the Innova Crysta with its reclining seats is the best choice.

Yes! Abhijeet Travels operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. An early morning departure (5–6 AM) gets you to Hyderabad comfortably by late morning or noon, avoiding peak city traffic. Book in advance on WhatsApp.
