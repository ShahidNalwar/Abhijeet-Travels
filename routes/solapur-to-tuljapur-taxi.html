Solapur to Tuljapur is approximately 46 km by road. The journey takes around 1 hour — one of the most convenient same-day darshan trips from Solapur.

Yes, same-day return trips are the most popular option for Tuljapur. The driver will wait while you complete your Tulja Bhavani Mata darshan and return with you. Round trip Sedan starts at just ₹3,500.

Yes, for round trip bookings the driver waits at Tuljapur at no extra charge. Darshan wait times vary — on regular days it takes 1 to 2 hours, while on festival days like Navratri it can be longer. Inform the driver of your expected duration.
