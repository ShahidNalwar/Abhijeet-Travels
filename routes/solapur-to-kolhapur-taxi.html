Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Solapur to Kolhapur cab booking.

A Sedan (Dzire/Amaze) starts at ₹4,800 one-way and ₹7,200 round trip. SUV (Ertiga) starts at ₹5,100 one-way and ₹7,600 round trip. Premium SUV (Innova/Crysta) starts at ₹6,900 one-way and ₹10,500 round trip. Prices exclude toll and parking.

Solapur to Kolhapur is approximately 230 km by road via NH-548 through Sangli. The journey typically takes 4 to 5 hours under normal traffic conditions.

The cab travels via NH-548 through Kurduwadi, Miraj and Sangli before reaching Kolhapur. It's a smooth, well-maintained highway route. You can be dropped at any location in Kolhapur including Mahalaxmi Temple, Rankala Lake, bus stand or railway station.

For 1–4 passengers, the Sedan (Dzire/Amaze) is the most economical. For families or pilgrimage groups of 5–6, the SUV (Ertiga) is ideal. For premium comfort or larger families of 7, the Innova Crysta is the best choice.
