Everything you need to know about Solapur to Pune cab booking.

A Sedan (Dzire/Amaze) starts at ₹4,000 one-way and ₹6,600 round trip. SUV (Ertiga) starts at ₹4,800 one-way. Premium SUV (Innova/Crysta) starts at ₹6,500 one-way. Prices exclude toll and parking.

Solapur to Pune is approximately 250 km by road via NH-65. The journey takes around 4 to 5 hours under normal traffic conditions.

The fare includes driver charges, fuel, AC, and GST. Toll charges, state tax, and parking fees are extra and paid directly at booths during the journey.

Yes! Abhijeet Travels operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Early morning departures (4 AM, 5 AM) and late night bookings are handled regularly.
